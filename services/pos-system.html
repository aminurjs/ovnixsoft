POS System Solutions

At Ovnixsoft, we build efficient and scalable POS (Point of Sale) systems that simplify retail and restaurant operations. Whether you're a startup or an established business, we tailor your POS to fit your exact workflow.

Our team of developers crafts reliable, real-time POS systems using modern technologies like JavaScript, React JS, Node JS, Next.js, PHP, and Laravel — ensuring seamless and secure operations across web and desktop.

Service Overview

We deliver end-to-end POS systems tailored for retail, restaurants, and service-based industries. From product scanning, real-time inventory, sales reporting, to secure payment handling — everything is integrated for smooth daily operations.

  • + Custom POS Web & Desktop Apps
  • + Multi-Store & Multi-User Support
  • + Offline & Cloud Sync Capabilities
  • + Inventory & Sales Tracking
  • + Receipt Printing Integration
  • + Role-Based Access & User Management

Key Features

Our POS solutions offer real-time sales tracking, barcode scanning, employee shifts management, tax control, stock alerts, and intuitive dashboards. Whether you need a desktop app for in-store sales or a cloud-based system — we’ve got you covered.

From small shops to multi-branch enterprises, our POS systems are built to handle high-volume transactions with ease. Plus, you get detailed analytics and reporting to help you make smarter business decisions.

We also support hardware integration like barcode scanners, cash drawers, and receipt printers. Built using clean code and the latest tech stack — React, Next.js, Node.js, and Laravel — your POS system is fast, stable, and future-ready.
