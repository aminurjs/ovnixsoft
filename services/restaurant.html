Restaurant Management System

Ovnixsoft builds smart, modern Restaurant Management Systems to streamline your operations — from dine-in orders to kitchen management, reservations to billing. We offer both single and multi-branch solutions.

Developed with technologies like Laravel, React, Next.js, and Node.js — our restaurant systems are user-friendly, fast, and tailored to your restaurant’s needs, whether it's a small café or a multi-location franchise.

Service Overview

We create restaurant systems that simplify every aspect of your business: table orders, online delivery, kitchen display systems, staff management, and inventory tracking — all in one platform.

Ovnixsoft's solution is scalable — ideal for restaurants, fast food chains, food trucks, or fine dining operations. Our responsive interfaces ensure great experiences for waiters, chefs, and managers alike.

  • + Dine-In, Takeaway, & Delivery Management
  • + Kitchen Display System (KDS)
  • + Online Reservation System
  • + POS & Billing Integration
  • + Menu & Modifier Management
  • + Stock & Ingredient Tracking
